Elementary Education in India

In the year 1950 the Constitution in Article 45 under the Directive Principles of State Policy resolved that “….State shall endeavour to provide, within a period of ten years from the commencement of this Constitution, for free and compulsory education to all children till they complete the age of fourteen”. Thereafter many Constitutional amendments were made with intent to give a boost to elementary education in India.Education creates opportunities for the children living in leprosy colonies.
